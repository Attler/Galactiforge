Space shooter with on-ship factories.

Features
- Ships with grids and factories
- Procedural resource management
- Conveyors and pipelines
- Click to place factories/weapons etc
- Weapon and ammo variety
- Thruster fuel production and variety
- Weapon, thruster, shields etc stats are determined by the ship factory capabilities
